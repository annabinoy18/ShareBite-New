About

Welcome to ShareBite, a community-driven platform dedicated to reducing food waste and fighting hunger. We connect individuals, catering services, and organizations like orphanages and shelters to ensure that surplus food reaches those who need it most.

Our Mission

"Share a meal, Spread a smile!"


At ShareBite, our mission is simple: to create a world where no meal goes to waste and everyone has access to nutritious food. We believe that by bringing people together, we can make a real difference in the lives of those facing food insecurity.

Operation

Have leftover food from a function or surplus from your kitchen? Simply log in, provide the details, and we'll help you find nearby organizations or individuals in need. Your generous donation can go a long way in making a difference.
